September 26, 2015

Saw project at HTPGDIY and tried to duplicate it.
Noticed it was a variation of the wire version at ruckman.net.
Decided to use foil version since I have no spare 10awg wire.
Printed out the PDF file on A6 (8 inch by 11 inch) paper and measured to confirm 1 inch long smaller segments and 4 inch long larger segments.
Glued tinfoil on back of paper and cut designs out with sharp scissors. (Dull razor blade would tear the paper)
Glued designs to a piece of plastic cut out from old plastic packaging. Separated designs by 1/4 of an inch.
Attached matching transformer (Balun) from TheSource to plastic design at the rounded ends using nuts and bolts.
Connected to coax and it worked! (Unfortunately only received one channel, CBUT-DT (UHF 43) reliably)
